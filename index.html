Sabir Kakar
Boxer & Martial Artist

Sabir Kakar was born in a remote village of Balochistan, Pakistan, Tehsil Gulistan, District Qila Abdullah, village Abdurahmanzai.

Growing up in a rural area with limited opportunities, life was full of challenges from an early age. To support himself and his family, he started working as a shopkeeper, learning responsibility, discipline, and patience at a young age.

Despite the hardships, Sabir carried a strong passion for martial arts, boxing, and kickboxing. With no proper facilities and after long working hours, he still pushed himself to train, driven by his dream to become stronger and compete.

Through relentless effort, consistency, and belief in himself, Alhamdulillah, Sabir Kakar has now become a kickboxer representing Pakistan 🇵🇰 — a proud achievement not only for himself but for his village and community.

His journey is not just a story of success, but a message — that no matter where you come from, with determination, faith, and hard work, you can rise and achieve greatness

He excelled Nationally by winning gold medals at National Championships in Kickboxing and with his aggressive fighting style became popularly known as 'Nisar Boxer' within martial arts circles in Pakistan. Later, he became inclined to learn Muay Thai, and included this in his Martial Arts repertoire.

In 2023, Nisar joined AKS-Dojo™ to further enhance his Martial Arts career through professional growth and personal development.

AKS
AKS-Dojo™ — Here he learnt athletic discipline, and enhanced his skills by following a strict training regimen. He gained knowledge of mental and physical well-being, which enabled him to take his career to new heights.

In 2024, his dream was fulfilled when he was crowned Muay Thai World Champion at the World Muay Federation Pro-Amateur Muay Thai Championship in Bangkok, Thailand.

Nisar became the first fighter from Pakistan to win this Pro-Amateur title, which provided impetus to his life's Martial Arts journey. This was a special and memorable win for him; it gave him a sense of deep satisfaction with joy given his life's challenging circumstances.

With over 200 fights in the ring, Nisar ranks amongst the top Martial Arts athletes in Pakistan, and has won many titles and gold medals. He has passionately raised Pakistan's flag abroad through his fight wins, and brought back laurels for the country by winning titles and medals in Russia, Kazakhstan, Thailand and Azerbaijan.
