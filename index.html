Sabir Kakar
Boxer & Martial Artist

Sabir Kakar was born in a remote village of Balochistan, Pakistan, Tehsil Gulistan, District Qila Abdullah, village Abdurahmanzai.

Growing up in a rural area with limited opportunities, life was full of challenges from an early age. To support himself and his family, he started working as a shopkeeper, learning responsibility, discipline, and patience at a young age.

Despite the hardships, Sabir carried a strong passion for martial arts, boxing, and kickboxing. With no proper facilities and after long working hours, he still pushed himself to train, driven by his dream to become stronger and compete.

Through relentless effort, consistency, and belief in himself, Alhamdulillah, Sabir Kakar has now become a kickboxer representing Pakistan 🇵🇰 — a proud achievement not only for himself but for his village and community.

His journey is not just a story of success, but a message — that no matter where you come from, with determination, faith, and hard work, you can rise and achieve greatness

Sabir Kakar emerged as one of Pakistan’s most promising martial artists, earning recognition nationwide through his outstanding performances and championship victories. With his fearless approach and aggressive fighting style, he has built a strong reputation in combat sports.

Driven by passion and a hunger to grow, Sabir mastered multiple martial arts disciplines, including Kickboxing and Muay Thai, shaping himself into a complete and versatile fighter.

SABIR
Through relentless hard work and dedication, Sabir achieved remarkable success on international platforms. He proudly represented Pakistan and won gold medals in Afghanistan, Russia, and Kazakhstan, bringing honor and recognition to his country.

On the national level, Sabir has proven his dominance by becoming a 4-time Pakistan National Champion — a testament to his consistency, discipline, and elite performance.

With over 200 fights in Kickboxing and other combat disciplines, Sabir’s experience inside the ring reflects his dedication, endurance, and fighting spirit.

Today, Sabir Kakar stands among the top martial artists in Pakistan. His journey reflects determination, resilience, and an unbreakable spirit — inspiring the next generation to rise, fight, and achieve greatness.
